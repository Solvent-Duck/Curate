About Curate

Curate is a powerful Firefox extension that helps you maintain control over your web browsing experience by filtering out unwanted content based on your personal blacklist of terms. Whether you want to avoid spoilers, filter out certain topics, or simply create a more focused browsing environment, Curate makes it easy to customize what you see online.

Smart Filtering

Automatically detects and hides content containing your blacklisted terms across all websites.

Real-time Updates

Changes to your blacklist are applied immediately across all open tabs without requiring page refreshes.

Dynamic Content Support

Filters content that loads dynamically, including social media feeds and single-page applications.

Privacy Focused

All filtering happens locally in your browser. Your blacklist is stored locally and never sent to external servers.

Easy Management

Simple popup interface for quick access to add, remove, or clear blacklisted terms.

Case Insensitive

Terms are matched regardless of case, ensuring comprehensive filtering across different text formats.

How It Works

Curate uses advanced content detection algorithms to scan web pages for your blacklisted terms. When matches are found, the containing elements are seamlessly hidden from view, making it appear as if the content was never there. The extension works across all websites and handles both static and dynamically loaded content.

Getting Started

To start using Curate, simply click the extension icon in your browser toolbar. From there, you can:

  • Add terms to your blacklist by typing them in the input field
  • Remove individual terms by clicking the "Remove" button next to them
  • Clear your entire blacklist using the "Clear All" button
  • See immediate results as content is filtered across all open tabs
